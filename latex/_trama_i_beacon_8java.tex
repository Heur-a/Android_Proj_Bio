\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
This class represents the structure of an i\+Beacon frame. 

The i\+Beacon frame is typically used for proximity marketing and location-\/based services, and consists of multiple fields such as a UUID, major and minor numbers, and signal strength. This class extracts and stores these fields from the raw byte data of a Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) scan.
